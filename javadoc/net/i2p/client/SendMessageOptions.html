Class SendMessageOptions

net.i2p.data.DataStructureImpl

net.i2p.client.SendMessageOptions
All Implemented Interfaces:
Serializable, DataStructure
public class SendMessageOptions
extends DateAndFlags
Advanced options attached to a single outgoing I2CP message. Note that the packing of options into the 16-bit flags field is is subject to change. Therefore, for now, this is only recommended within RouterContext. Static methods are for OutboundClientMessageOneShotJob to decode the flags field on the router side. GzipOption flags are as of 0.9.36, are client-side only, and are not included in the flags field or sent to the router.

0.9.2
